About the Conference

A premier academic & industry-focused event

ACCES-2025 aims to address transformative trends by fostering collaboration and disseminating groundbreaking research in communications, computing and electronic systems. The conference brings together research focused on sustainable electronics, computing models and communication technologies to develop novel solutions for emerging challenges.

Held in hybrid mode on 26 April 2025, it serves as a global meeting point for researchers, engineers, academicians and professionals from across the world.

Host — MLEW

Malineni Lakshmaiah Women's Engineering College, founded in 2008 to advance women's technical education. AICTE-approved, ISO 9001:2015 certified and affiliated with JNTU-K. The campus features computer centres, libraries, advanced labs, seminar halls and digital classrooms.

Technical Sponsor — UCSI University

Ranked 265th in the QS World University Rankings 2025 and among the top 1% of universities worldwide for the third consecutive year. Also ranked 61st in the QS Asia University Rankings 2024.

Technical Partner

Lived Edutech Services serves as the official technical partner for organizing the conference, supporting submissions, review and proceedings.

Call for Papers

Submit your original research

The conference invites original, unpublished manuscripts. Authors submit directly by email; accepted & presented papers appear in the conference proceedings, with select papers in Scopus-indexed venues.

Manuscript guidelines

  • A4 page format, single column layout
  • 11-point Times New Roman font
  • 1.5 line spacing
  • Submit as a Microsoft Word document
  • Numbered referencing style (not APA)
  • Length: 12–15 pages maximum
  • Abstract of 150–200 words

Where your work appears

Review & publication process

The conference requires original, unpublished research. All submissions undergo plagiarism screening with an acceptable limit of 15% — articles exceeding this threshold face immediate rejection. Non-plagiarized submissions go to two reviewers; the convenor and programme committee make acceptance decisions based on reviewer feedback.

Accepted and presented papers appear in Conference Proceedings, with select papers published in Scopus-indexed venues.

Note: Authors have to pay the APC applicable as per the journal guidelines for journal publication.

Previous proceedings: printorders.aip.org/proceedings/2794
